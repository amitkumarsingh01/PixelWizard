Welcome to the SLIET Mentorship Platform

At Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology (SLIET), we believe in empowering students through continuous academic and career guidance. Our dedicated mentorship platform connects students with faculty mentors who are committed to supporting their growth and success. Whether you're looking for academic advice, career guidance, or skill-building opportunities, our mentors are here to help you navigate your journey.

Why Mentorship Matters

Mentorship is a powerful way to enhance learning, gain insights, and accelerate career development. Through one-on-one sessions with experienced faculty, students can ask questions, seek advice, and gain invaluable knowledge that goes beyond the classroom.

Our mentorship platform is designed to foster meaningful interactions between faculty and students, helping you gain clarity on your academic path, sharpen your skills, and prepare for the future.

How It Works

Our platform helps students connect with mentors and easily book personalized sessions based on their availability.

  • Automated Booking

Mentor Availability: Faculty mentors offer time slots for mentorship sessions, regularly updated based on their schedules.

Booking Status: Available slots are clearly shown, while booked sessions are marked as “booked.”

Easy Booking: Students can select available slots and book directly. Mentors receive reminders for upcoming sessions.

  • Faculty Profiles

Mentors are experienced Computer Science faculty, offering guidance in various academic and career areas.

  • Expertise: Detailed profiles show their academic background, research, and achievements.
  • Areas of Guidance: Includes programming, research, internships, and career advice.
  • Availability: Students can view and book slots that fit their schedule.
